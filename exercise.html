Let's Build It: A Tiny Net From Scratch
Two layers of weights, two activation steps — that's the whole brain.
simple_net.py
class SimpleNet:
    def __init__(self):
        self.W1 = random_matrix(784, 128)
        self.W2 = random_matrix(128, 10)
    def forward(self, x):
        h = relu(x @ self.W1)
        return softmax(h @ self.W2)
